Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

EMI Group ("we," "our," or "us") operates the EMI Group mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone Number: Used for OTP-based authentication and login.
  • Name & Email: Optional profile information you may provide.
  • Profile Photo: Optional avatar image stored securely.
  • Payment Information: UPI transaction references for tracking EMI payments. We do not store banking credentials.
  • Push Notification Token: For sending payment reminders and group updates.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate and identify users
  • To manage group EMI schemes
  • To track payment status and history
  • To send reminders and notifications about due payments
  • To provide admin analytics and group management features

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas cloud servers with encryption at rest. We use industry-standard security practices including JWT token-based authentication and HTTPS encryption for all data transfers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Group members can see only your name and payment status within their group. Admins can access member information for group management purposes.

5. UPI Payments

EMI payments are processed through your preferred UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.). We only store transaction reference numbers for tracking purposes and never process or store your banking details.

6. Push Notifications

We use Expo Push Notification service to send payment reminders and group updates. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

7. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Upon request, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@emigroup.app
